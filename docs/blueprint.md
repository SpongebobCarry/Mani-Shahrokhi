# **App Name**: Portofolio

## Core Features:

- Personalized Introduction: A clean, inviting hero section prominently featuring the user's name, title, and a professional photo or avatar.
- About Me & Skills Showcase: A concise overview of the user's background, professional journey, and a clear, visual display of key skills and technologies.
- Project Gallery: Dedicated sections for displaying individual projects with titles, brief descriptions, images/videos, and direct links to live demos or repositories.
- Contact Information: Clear and easily accessible contact details (email, social media links) or a simple, functional contact form.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated, slightly muted blue, promoting trust and professionalism (#4D91B3).
- Background color: An almost-white, cool gray tone for a clean, open feel, providing excellent contrast (#F0F3F4).
- Accent color: A vibrant, analogous teal-green that adds a touch of creative energy and visual interest to interactive elements and highlights (#22C3A8).
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a proportional sans-serif with a techy and modern feel for titles and prominent text.
- Body font: 'Inter', a neutral grotesque-style sans-serif for clear and highly readable body text, complementing the headlines.
- Code font: 'Source Code Pro', a monospaced sans-serif, suitable for displaying code snippets or technical references.
- Utilize modern, clean, line-based icons to maintain a minimalist aesthetic and enhance navigability without visual clutter.
- Implement a minimalist, grid-based layout for showcasing projects, ensuring ample white space to draw focus to content and facilitate easy scanning.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and gentle on-scroll reveal effects to enhance user engagement and provide a polished, contemporary feel without being distracting.
